My Projects

Vanilla Javascript RPG Game: I did this as the final project of my General Assembly Course. I chose to do this to strengthen my OOP/DOM skills and to make something cool! There is no title, but you are a dwarf and the objective is to walk to the end of the level on the other side of the map. A goblin will randomly appear and attack you. Click on image to open. Note: only works in desktop.
Spotify Clone: I built this project for the group project in the General Assembly course. This revolutionary (lol) music app will allow users to create playlists from randomly curated favorites by genre. The frontend was build in React. The backend was done in express and MongoDB and is hosted on Heroku.
